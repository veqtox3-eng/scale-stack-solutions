Overview
Introduction

Welcome to ScaleStack Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with responsibility, transparency, and care.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information obtained through our website, services, and communications. By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Please read this policy carefully. If you have any questions or concerns, you can contact us at any time using the details provided in Section 11.

🔒 Our commitment: We only collect information that is necessary to deliver our services and improve your experience. We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Usage
How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for legitimate business purposes to deliver and improve our services. We use your information to:

  • Respond promptly to your inquiries, messages, and contact form submissions
  • Deliver our website development, paid advertising, and AI automation services
  • Improve the performance, content, and user experience of our website
  • Send relevant service updates, newsletters, and promotional communications (only with your consent)
  • Analyze website traffic and measure the effectiveness of our marketing campaigns
  • Provide ongoing customer support and account management
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect against fraudulent activity
  • Personalize your experience and tailor our services to your business needs
📬

We may send you marketing communications only when you have explicitly opted in. You can unsubscribe from any marketing email at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or contacting us directly.

Tracking Technologies
Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience, analyze site traffic, and support our marketing activities. A cookie is a small text file placed on your device when you visit our website.

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function correctly. Cannot be disabled. Session
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website (e.g. Google Analytics). Up to 2 years
Marketing Cookies Used to track visitors across websites to display relevant advertisements. Up to 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your preferences and settings to personalize your experience. Up to 1 year

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymized data about website usage, including page views, session duration, and user behavior. This data helps us improve our website and services.

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings or by using a cookie management tool. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Protection Measures
Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

🔒

SSL Encryption

All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/SSL protocols.

🛡️

Access Controls

Access to personal data is strictly limited to authorized personnel who need it to perform their role.

🔄

Regular Reviews

We regularly review and update our security practices to stay ahead of evolving threats.

🗄️

Secure Storage

Personal data is stored on secure, password-protected systems with restricted access protocols.

🧹

Data Minimization

We only collect and retain data that is necessary — nothing more than what our services require.

📋

Staff Training

Our team receives regular training on data protection best practices and privacy obligations.

⚠️

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of data transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability and will notify you promptly in the event of any breach that affects your data.

7
Storage Duration
Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as is necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

  • Contact inquiries: Retained for up to 2 years to provide ongoing support and follow-up.
  • Client data: Retained for the duration of our engagement and for a reasonable period thereafter for legal and operational purposes.
  • Analytics data: Aggregated, anonymized usage data may be retained indefinitely to identify long-term trends.
  • Marketing data: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion, whichever comes first.
  • Legal records: Certain records may be retained longer if required by law or regulatory obligations.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it in accordance with our internal data management procedures. You may request early deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8 – Your Rights).

8
Your Control
Your Rights

You have important rights regarding your personal information. We are committed to honoring these rights promptly and transparently. Depending on your location and applicable privacy laws, you may have the right to:

👁️

Right of Access

Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you and how it is being used.

✏️

Right to Correction

Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information held about you.

🗑️

Right to Deletion

Request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations we must fulfill.

🚫

Right to Object

Object to the processing of your data for marketing or other purposes at any time.

📤

Data Portability

Request a copy of your data in a machine-readable format where technically feasible.

↩️

Withdraw Consent

Withdraw your consent for data processing at any time where consent is the legal basis.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@scalestacksolutions.com. We will respond to all requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request.

9
Age Restrictions
Children's Privacy

ScaleStack Solutions' website and services are intended for business owners, professionals, and adults. Our services are not directed at or intended for children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our records.

👶

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us without your consent, please contact us immediately at privacy@scalestacksolutions.com so we can remove it promptly.

Changes To This Policy

ScaleStack Solutions may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business practices, legal requirements, or the services we offer. We are committed to keeping this policy accurate and up to date.

  • Any material changes to this policy will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
  • For significant changes that materially affect how we use your personal information, we will make reasonable efforts to notify you — such as by email or a notice on our website.
  • Your continued use of our website or services after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of the revised terms.
